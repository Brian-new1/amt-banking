How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password by clicking "Forgot Password" on the login screen and following the instructions.

How do I open an online banking account?

You can open an account by clicking the "Sign In" button on our Home page and following the registration process. You'll need a valid ID, proof of address, and your account information.

What are the fees for using this account?

Fees vary by account type. Please refer to our pricing page or contact support for specific details.

How do I update my personal details?

You can update your address, phone number, or email by logging in to your account and navigating to Settings > Personal Information, or by visiting your nearest branch with a valid ID.

How do I close my account?

To close your account, please contact our customer support team or visit a branch. Make sure all pending transactions are completed and your balance is cleared before submitting your closure request.

How do I make an online transfer?

You can transfer funds by logging into your account and selecting "Transfers." Follow the on-screen instructions.

How can I pay my bills through online banking?

Navigate to the "Payments" section, choose the biller, and enter your account details to complete the payment.

How do I set up a recurring payment?

Log in to online banking, choose the payee, select the payment frequency (e.g., weekly or monthly), and confirm the schedule. You can manage or cancel recurring payments anytime in your payment settings.

What is the maximum amount I can transfer online?

Transfer limits vary depending on your account type and security settings. You can view your current limit in Settings > Transfer Limits or by contacting our support team.

Can I cancel a payment after it’s been made?

Once a payment is processed, it usually can’t be reversed. If it’s still pending, you can cancel it via the “Pending Transactions” page or by calling us immediately.

Is mobile banking secure?

Yes. Our mobile banking app uses encryption and multifactor authentication to keep your information safe.

What should I do if I notice suspicious activity on my account?

Immediately contact our support team and change your password. We'll help you investigate and secure your account.

How do I enable two-factor authentication (2FA)?

You can turn on 2FA in your account security settings. Once enabled, you’ll need to enter a one-time code sent to your phone or email each time you log in.

How do you protect my personal information?

We use advanced encryption, secure servers, and regular security monitoring to protect your personal and financial data.

How can I recognise a phishing email or scam message?

Look for poor grammar, urgent payment requests, or suspicious links. We’ll never ask for your password or PIN via email or text. Report any suspicious messages to our security team immediately.
