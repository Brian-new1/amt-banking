Frequently Asked Questions

How do I open a new bank account? +
You can open a new bank account online by filling out our application form and providing the required identification documents, or visit your nearest branch for in-person assistance.
What is the minimum balance requirement? +
The minimum balance requirement varies depending on the account type. For our standard savings account, the minimum balance is $100.
How can I reset my online banking password? +
You can reset your password by clicking "Forgot Password" on the login page and following the verification steps. You may also contact customer support for assistance.
Are my online transactions secure? +
Yes, all online transactions are protected with advanced encryption and multi-factor authentication to ensure your account remains safe and secure.
